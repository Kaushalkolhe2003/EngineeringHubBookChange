About Us

EngineeringHub BookXChange is designed for college students to easily buy, sell, and share books and study materials. Our platform connects pass-out students with current students across departments like Computer Engineering and Electronics & Telecommunication.

Our Services

  • Buy & Sell Second-Hand Books
  • Share Study Materials and Notes
  • Connect Directly with Sellers and Buyers
  • Department-Wise Listings for Easy Access

Why Use EngineeringHub BookXChange?

Save Money: Affordable second-hand books for students on a budget.

Easy Access: Quickly find books or materials for your department and semester.

Eco-Friendly: Promote recycling and reduce waste by reusing books.

Community Driven: Connect with fellow students and alumni easily.
